## About

I work as an IT Lead in the printing industry, focusing on software that supports real operational needs. My work is centered on internal systems, business operations, e-commerce workflows, logistics tooling, and product execution across multiple teams and processes.

I am most comfortable where technical leadership and hands-on building meet: leading teams, shaping delivery direction, designing systems across divisions, migrating platforms, and building products from the ground up. Across the systems connected to this profile, the strongest recurring stack is TypeScript, Next.js, React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Supabase, and Tailwind CSS.

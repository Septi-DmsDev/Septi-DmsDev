## About

I work at the intersection of operations, product execution, and engineering. My focus is on turning business workflows into dependable software—especially across HR systems, payroll support, attendance flows, internal approvals, IT ticketing, and business-facing platforms. Across the repositories connected to this profile, the strongest recurring stack is TypeScript, Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS, Supabase, and PostgreSQL, with Astro and Node.js used where they best support delivery.
